AIStain Model Demo

A pre-trained and subsequently fine-tuned model was developed to translate images of stained H&E slides into images of stained CD3 slides. This project was developed under the guidance of Professor Condon Lau at the CityU of Hong Kong and was awarded silver at the 4th Asia Exhibition of Innovations and Inventions (2024).
